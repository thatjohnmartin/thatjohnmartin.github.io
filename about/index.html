About Me

These days, I design and build things at YuMe, a digital video advertising behemoth that acquired my startup, Crowd Science. I founded Crowd Science in 2008 with John Wainwright and Paul Neto and we built machine learning-based prediction engines for online publishers.

Prior to founding Crowd Science I was part of the engineering team at comScore and Cisco as well as a handful of startups in Sydney and Toronto.

I have a degree in Computer Science from the University of Sydney but spent most of my time doing Philosophy. It turns out that free will does not exist.

Also, I like to climb rocks.
